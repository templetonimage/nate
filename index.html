The Story
Built from legacy.

On location · Templeton Image

I'm Nate Templeton — a videographer, community builder, conference organizer and solopreneur based out of Baltimore with growing roots in Chicago.

My primary work is cinematic wedding and promotional videography under Templeton Image. I specialize in destination weddings for couples who see their film as an heirloom, not just a keepsake. On the promotional side, I work with conferences, events, and small businesses that want video content with real creative intentionality behind it.

Outside of client work, I run and co-organize The Peculiar Collective — a thriving photo and video community in the Baltimore/D.C. area — and help produce The Peculiar Conference, now in its fifth year. I also build accountability & consistency programs for creatives serious about growth plus build custom platforms for other creatives, and host a business book club.

"Solopreneurship can be isolating — the late nights, the self-doubt, the feeling that everyone else has it figured out. That's why community isn't optional. Finding your people changes everything. You don't have to go at this alone."
