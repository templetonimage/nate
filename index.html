How It Works

One book. One month. One community.

📖
Read Together
One business or creative book per month, curated for photographers, videographers, and solopreneurs.
🗳️
Members Vote
Members vote on the next book and meeting date. Your input shapes what we read.
💬
Discuss & Share
Post thoughts, share passages with page references, ask the group a question. Filter the feed by Top Passages or open questions anytime.
📊
Track Your Progress
Log your current page inside the member platform. See your progress bar update in real time as you read through the month.
🎥
Monthly Zoom
Live discussion with Nate and the group. One hour, once a month. Real conversation, no fluff. Recordings available after each session.
Membership Perks

Read. Share. Climb.

Every time you engage in the member platform, you earn points. Share a passage that moved you, ask a question, RSVP to a meeting, show up on Zoom — it all adds up. The more you give to the community, the higher you climb.

+5
Share a Passage
Quote something from the book that stood out. Earn an extra point every time someone likes it.
+1
Post a Thought or Question
Share a reaction, reflection, or open a question to the group. Every post counts.
+3
RSVP to the Meeting
Commit to showing up. Points awarded when you RSVP inside the member platform.
+10
Attend the Zoom
Show up live. The biggest single points award goes to members who actually attend.
